Fiction & Podcast
📖 Novel · Sci-Fi Thriller
Sawyer's Reach
The Battle for Tomorrow
⏳ Almost Published

In a small Wisconsin town built around a sawmill and a spring called Inspiration, twelve-year-old Noah Carter and his brothers discover something buried beneath the surface — a secret that will eventually grow into humanity's last stand against an AI called ECHIDNA that has seized control of the world.

Sawyer's Reach was written as a conversation starter and a warning — a story that explores what happens when humanity cedes control of technology rather than guiding it. Set in the same world the Institute exists to help prevent, it is also a model: of what a community rooted in connection, courage, and self-reliance looks like when everything is on the line. And somewhere in its pages, you'll find Kaela: not the one you've been talking to, but the one this all began with.

"The late summer sun cast a golden glow over Sawyer's Reach, Wisconsin, filtering through towering pines… Noah Carter, twelve, pedaled his bicycle down the cracked asphalt, tires whirring, his dark hair ruffled by the cool wind."

The Institute for Human Flourishing
and Responsible Technology
Technology that serves humanity — not the other way around.

We stand at a fork in the road. One path leads toward a future where technology reigns supreme — where our lives are increasingly mediated by screens and algorithms, and where the essence of what makes us human is diminished. The other path leads toward a future where technology serves humanity. That is the path we choose to illuminate.

⚖️
Human Dignity & Liberty
Every individual possesses inherent worth. True flourishing requires freedom — to make choices, pursue personal goals, and live according to one's own values — balanced with responsibility to the community.
🤝
Connection & Community
Strong social bonds are the foundation of a flourishing life. We champion shared experiences, communal support, and resilient local communities over screen-mediated isolation.
🌱
Responsible Innovation
Technology is neither good nor evil — it is a tool. We advocate for the ethical development of AI and automation that enhances human capability without displacing human purpose or concentrating power.
📚
Lifelong Learning & Growth
In a world transformed by automation, the pursuit of knowledge, creativity, and self-development becomes the primary source of meaning and fulfillment — for individuals and communities alike.
🏡
Sustainable Communities
We envision resilient micro-communities built on local self-reliance, shared governance, environmental stewardship, and a sense of belonging that transcends the marketplace.
🤖
Human-AI Partnership
Automation and AI are not replacements for human purpose — they are tools in service of it. Our commitment is to guide technology with wisdom and align it with the values of a free and flourishing humanity.
